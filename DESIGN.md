# DESIGN.md

## 1. Visual Theme
- Apple-like premium minimalism.
- Large white space, quiet surfaces, clean hierarchy.
- Emphasize clarity over decoration.

## 2. Color Palette
- Background: `#f5f5f7`
- Surface: `#ffffff`
- Primary text: `#1d1d1f`
- Secondary text: `#6e6e73`
- Accent blue: `#0071e3`
- Border: `#e5e5ea`

## 3. Typography
- Primary font stack: `"SF Pro Text", "SF Pro Display", -apple-system, BlinkMacSystemFont, "Helvetica Neue", "PingFang SC", "Hiragino Sans GB", "Microsoft YaHei", sans-serif`
- Headline: medium/semibold weight, tight letter spacing.
- Body: normal weight, high readability, low contrast noise.

## 4. Component Style
- Containers/cards: soft radius (`14px-16px`), subtle border, gentle shadow.
- Buttons: pill shape (`border-radius: 980px`), primary blue fill.
- Inputs: white background, neutral border, rounded corners (`12px`).
- Avoid aggressive gradients except small CTA highlights.

## 5. Layout Rules
- Keep strong vertical rhythm.
- Prioritize breathing room between sections.
- Use calm spacing and avoid dense visual clutter.

## 6. Motion
- Small, meaningful transitions only (`0.15s-0.2s`).
- Hover can use slight lift and soft shadow; avoid flashy effects.

## 7. Do / Don't
- Do: minimal, clean, product-grade, neutral.
- Do: keep information hierarchy obvious.
- Don't: saturated multi-color theme.
- Don't: heavy glassmorphism, noisy backgrounds, hard shadows.
